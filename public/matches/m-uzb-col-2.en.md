## home_note

They won 15 of 16 qualifying matches with only one defeat, boasting a solid defensive record. Their most recognisable player is Manchester City centre-back Khusanov (the first Uzbek to play in the Premier League), while prolific striker Shomurodov (Roma, on loan at Istanbul) is the all-time national team top scorer with 44 goals. Operating in a 3-4-2-1 system, they prioritise disciplined defending and patient possession. Ranked 64th by FIFA, this match against Colombia is very much the underdog battle.

## away_note
