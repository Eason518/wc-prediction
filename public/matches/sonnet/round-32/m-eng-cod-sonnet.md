## home_note:en

Arsenal teammates Bukayo Saka and Leandro Trossard — though Trossard is Belgian — provide the attacking width. ## away_note:en
DR Congo return to the World Cup for the first time since 1974 (as Zaire) after a remarkable qualification campaign via inter-confederation play-offs. Coach Sébastien Desabre has built a diaspora-heavy, defensively organised unit anchored by captain and record cap-holder Chancel Mbemba (Lille, 107 caps). Their key attacking threat is Newcastle United winger Yoane Wissa — fast, lethal and PL-proven — alongside veteran striker Cédric Bakambu (Real Betis, 21 international goals). Aaron Wan-Bissaka (West Ham) and Axel Tuanzebe (Burnley) provide Premier League know-how in defence. DR Congo finished second in Group K with four points (beat Uzbekistan, drew with Portugal 1-1, lost to Colombia). They are well organised but a step short of England's quality level.

## summary_verdict:en
England are one of the tournament's favourites and face what should be their most straightforward Round of 32 fixture on paper. DR Congo's historic debut in the knockouts is a wonderful football story, but the gulf in squad quality is substantial. Kane (81 goals, 60-goal season), Bellingham (Champions League winner), and Saka (PL champion) represent a level of attacking quality that DR Congo have never faced in competitive football.

DR Congo will defend deep, use Wan-Bissaka's PL pedigree to limit Saka's space, and look for Wissa on the counter. That approach worked against Portugal (1-1) and kept Colombia at 0.98 xG, so there is a defensive template. But against England's range of creativity — Rice driving forward, Bellingham arriving late, Saka's dribbling and Kane's penalty-box menace — they are unlikely to keep a clean sheet for 90 minutes.

Prediction: 3-0 England win. Kane opens the scoring, Bellingham and Saka add late goals as DR Congo's legs tire in the Atlanta heat. A Wissa counter-attack or set-piece threat is the main danger, but England's defensive quality — Rice's screening and Stones/Guéhi's composure — should comfortably handle it.
